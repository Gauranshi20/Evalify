# User Personas

## Administrator

### Goals
- Manage the entire platform
- Manage users
- View institution analytics
- Configure the system

### Pain Points
- Difficult user management
- Lack of centralized reports

---

## Teacher

### Goals
- Create assignments
- Review submissions
- Publish grades quickly
- Save grading time using AI

### Pain Points
- Manual grading
- Repetitive feedback
- Time consuming evaluation

---

## Student

### Goals
- Submit assignments
- Receive timely feedback
- Track progress
- Improve learning

### Pain Points
- Delayed grading
- Lack of personalized feedback

---

## Parent

### Goals
- Monitor child's academic progress
- Receive important notifications
- View reports

### Pain Points
- Limited visibility into performance
